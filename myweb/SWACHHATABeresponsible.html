SWACHHATA AWARENESS PROGRAM

 Be Responsible

10 things  I can do to bring  SWACHHATA

  1. Divide house into different divisions and schedule to clean each one accordingly to ensure the maintenance of cleanliness at home , systematically.
  2. List out the places at Puducherry where wastes are dumped inappropriately with the help of my friends and bring it to the notice of near-by Municipality department.
  3. Educate females about menstrual hygiene and incineration of sanitary napkins.
  4. Set up composting bins and separate baskets for biodegradable and non biodegradable wastes at neighborhood to organize waste disposal.
  5. Commemorate a simple contest among my neighbors who will be judged upon their cleanliness at road by the surveillance of toddlers who play outdoors.
  6. Have a friendly meeting with school maids frequently to encourage them about their work and suggest a few ways to maintain cleanliness at school.
  7. Have separate bins for used masks and gloves.
  8. Warning signs saying NOT TO LITTER can be boring sometimes.Such signs👇 with a twist will urge the citizens to maintain SWACHHATA.
  9. Going for homemade cleaning agents such as turmeric solution , lime , saline and neem can make things affordable and chemical free for regular usage.
  10. Electric appliances including gadgets which are very handy these days can be sanitized regularly and brushed frequently to avoid untidiness.
